Garlic Parmesan Pasta

Garlic Parmesan Pasta is a comforting and flavorful dish that combines al dente pasta with a creamy sauce infused with garlic and Parmesan cheese. This dish is easy to prepare and perfect for a quick weeknight meal. The richness of the sauce paired with the savory notes of garlic and Parmesan creates a satisfying and delicious pasta dish that will please both kids and adults alike.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

instructions

step 1

Cook the Pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil over high heat. Add the pasta to the boiling water and cook according to package instructions until al dente, typically about 8 to 12 minutes depending on the type of pasta. Once the pasta is cooked, reserve about 1/2 cup of the pasta cooking water, then drain the pasta in a colander.

step 2

Prepare the Garlic Parmesan Sauce: While the pasta is cooking, heat a large skillet or saucepan over medium heat. Add the butter to the skillet and let it melt. Add the minced garlic to the skillet and sauté for about 1-2 minutes until fragrant, being careful not to brown the garlic.

step 3

Make the Creamy Sauce: Pour in the heavy cream (or half-and-half) into the skillet with the garlic and butter. Stir and bring the mixture to a simmer. Let it cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally, until slightly thickened.

step 4

Add Parmesan Cheese: Gradually add the grated Parmesan cheese to the skillet, stirring constantly, until the cheese is melted and the sauce is smooth and creamy. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the seasoning according to your preference.

step 5

Combine Pasta and Sauce: Add the cooked and drained pasta directly to the skillet with the creamy garlic Parmesan sauce. Toss the pasta in the sauce until well coated, using tongs or a pasta fork to combine. If the sauce is too thick, you can add a splash of the reserved pasta cooking water to loosen it up.

ingredients

8 ounces (about 225 grams) of pasta

3 tablespoons butter

4 cloves garlic, minced

1 cup heavy cream

1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Salt and pepper

Chopped fresh parsley, for garnish (optional)

tools

Large Pot

Skillet or Saucepan

Measuring Spoons and Cups

Tongs or Pasta Fork


Honey Soy Glazed Salmon

Honey soy glazed salmon is a delightful dish that combines the rich flavors of salmon with a sweet and savory glaze. The salmon fillets are generously coated with a mixture of honey, soy sauce, garlic, and ginger, which caramelizes beautifully when cooked. This glaze not only enhances the natural taste of the salmon but also adds a delicious depth of flavor. It's a perfect balance of sweet and salty, making it a favorite choice for seafood lovers looking for a simple yet impressive dish.

prep time

35 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

4 servings

instructions

step 1

Prepare the Glaze: In a small bowl, combine 1/4 cup of honey, 2 tablespoons of soy sauce, 1 tablespoon of minced garlic, and 1 teaspoon of grated ginger. Mix well to ensure all ingredients are incorporated.

step 2

Marinate the Salmon: Place salmon fillets (about 4-6 ounces each) in a shallow dish or resealable plastic bag. Pour the prepared honey soy glaze over the salmon, making sure it coats the fillets evenly. Allow the salmon to marinate in the glaze for at least 15-30 minutes. You can marinate longer for more flavor, up to 1-2 hours in the refrigerator

step 3

Preheat the Oven: While the salmon is marinating, preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

step 4

Bake the Salmon: Place the marinated salmon fillets onto the prepared baking sheet, skin-side down if they have skin. Reserve any extra glaze for basting during cooking. Bake the salmon in the preheated oven for about 12-15 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork. Cooking time may vary depending on the thickness of your fillets.

step 5

Baste with Glaze: About halfway through the cooking time, brush the salmon with additional glaze from the marinade for extra flavor and a glossy finish.

step 6

Preheat the Oven:Serve: Once the salmon is cooked to your liking, remove it from the oven. Serve the honey soy glazed salmon hot, garnished with chopped green onions or sesame seeds if desired.

ingredients

4 salmon fillets

1/4 cup honey

2 tablespoons soy sauce (use low-sodium soy sauce if preferred)

1 tablespoon minced garlic

1 teaspoon grated ginger

Optional Garnish: Chopped green onions or sesame seeds for garnish

tools

Baking Sheet or Dish

Mixing Bowl

Whisk or Spoon

Measuring Spoons and Cups

Oven

Knife and Cutting Board (for garnish)


Mushroom and Spinach Stuffed Chicken Breast

Classic mushroom and spinach stuffed chicken breast is an elegant and flavorful dish that features tender chicken breasts filled with a savory mixture of sautéed mushrooms, spinach, garlic, and melted cheese. This dish is both hearty and delicious, perfect for a special dinner occasion

prep time

25 min.

cook time

30 min.

serving

2-4 servings

instructions

step 1

Prepare the Filling: In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Sauté sliced mushrooms until golden brown, then add minced garlic and spinach. Cook until spinach wilts. Season with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning (if desired). Remove from heat and let cool slightly.

step 2

Prepare Chicken Breasts: Carefully slice a pocket into each chicken breast. Season the inside and outside of the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.

step 3

Stuff the Chicken: Stuff each chicken breast with the cooled mushroom and spinach mixture. Add a layer of shredded mozzarella or Parmesan cheese on top of the filling.

step 4

Secure the Chicken: Use cooking twine or toothpicks to secure the openings of the stuffed chicken breasts to prevent the filling from leaking out.

step 5

Cook the Chicken: Place the stuffed chicken breasts in a baking dish. Bake in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for about 25-30 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).

step 6

Serve: Remove the cooked stuffed chicken breasts from the oven. Let them rest for a few minutes before slicing. Serve hot with your favorite side dishes.

ingredients

Chicken breasts (boneless and skinless)

Mushrooms

Fresh spinach leaves

Garlic, minced

Shredded mozzarella or Parmesan cheese

Olive oil

Salt and pepper

Italian seasoning (optional)

tools

Frying pan or skillet

Baking dish

Cooking twine or toothpicks (for securing chicken)

Oven preheated to 375°F (190°C)


Classic Margherita Pizza

A classic Margherita pizza is a simple yet flavorful Italian dish featuring a thin crust topped with tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella cheese, fresh basil leaves, and a drizzle of olive oil. It's a timeless favorite known for its vibrant colors and delicious combination of ingredients.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

12 min.

serving

3 servings

instructions

step 1

Preheat the Oven: Place a pizza stone or baking sheet in the oven and preheat to 475°F to 500°F (245°C to 260°C).

step 2

Prepare the Dough: Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.

step 3

Top the Pizza: Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Arrange slices or pieces of fresh mozzarella evenly over the sauce.

step 4

Add Basil Leaves: Scatter fresh basil leaves over the cheese.

step 5

Drizzle with Olive Oil: Lightly drizzle olive oil over the pizza.

step 6

Bake the Pizza: Carefully transfer the pizza onto the preheated pizza stone or baking sheet. Bake for about 10-12 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.

step 5

Slice and Serve: Remove the pizza from the oven, let it cool slightly, then slice it into wedges. Serve hot and enjoy!

ingredients

Pizza dough (homemade or store-bought)

Tomato sauce or marinara sauce

Fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced or torn

Fresh basil leaves

Olive oil

Salt and pepper (optional)

tools

Pizza stone or baking sheet

Rolling pin (if shaping your own dough)

Oven preheated to a high temperature (typically around 475°F to 500°F or 245°C to 260°C)

Pizza cutter or sharp knife


Cajun Shrimp Skillet

Cajun shrimp skillet is a zesty and flavorful dish that features succulent shrimp cooked in a spicy Cajun seasoning along with bell peppers, onions, and garlic. This one-pan meal is quick to prepare and perfect for a weeknight dinner, offering bold and delicious Cajun flavors.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

10 min.

serving

2-4 servings

instructions

step 1

Season the Shrimp: In a bowl, toss the shrimp with Cajun seasoning until well coated.

step 2

Sauté Vegetables: Heat olive oil or butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add sliced bell peppers and onions. Cook until softened and slightly charred.

step 3

Cook Shrimp: Push the vegetables to the side of the skillet and add minced garlic. Stir until fragrant, then add the seasoned shrimp to the skillet. Cook for 2-3 minutes per side or until the shrimp turn pink and opaque.

step 4

Combine and Serve: Stir the cooked shrimp and vegetables together. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with fresh parsley.

step 5

Serve: Serve the Cajun shrimp skillet hot over cooked rice or with crusty bread for a delicious and satisfying meal.

ingredients

Shrimp, peeled and deveined

Cajun seasoning

Bell peppers (red and green), sliced

Onion, sliced

Garlic, minced

Olive oil or butter

Salt and pepper

Fresh parsley (for garnish)

Cooked rice or crusty bread (for serving)

tools

Large skillet or frying pan

Baking dish

Wooden spoon or spatula for stirring

Serving platter or individual plates
