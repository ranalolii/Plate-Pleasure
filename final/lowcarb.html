Caprese Chicken Skillet

Caprese Chicken Skillet is a delightful and fresh dish that combines juicy chicken breasts with classic Caprese salad ingredients—tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil. This one-pan meal is bursting with flavors of Italy and is perfect for a quick and satisfying dinner.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

20 min.

serving

2-4 servings

instructions

step 1

Preheat the Oven: If finishing in the oven, preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).

step 2

Season the Chicken: Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper on both sides.

step 3

Sear the Chicken: Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Sear the chicken breasts until golden brown on both sides and cooked through (about 6-8 minutes per side).

step 4

Add Tomatoes and Mozzarella: Add halved cherry tomatoes to the skillet around the chicken. Top each chicken breast with slices of fresh mozzarella cheese.

step 5

Finish Cooking (Optional): If using an oven-safe skillet, transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for 5-7 minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

step 6

Garnish and Serve: Remove from the oven. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and drizzle with balsamic vinegar. Serve hot.

  • Calories: 320 per serving
  • Total Fat: 15g
  • Saturated Fat: 5g
  • Cholesterol: 85mg
  • Sodium: 540mg
  • Total Carbohydrates: 4g
  • Dietary Fiber: 1g
  • Sugars: 2g
  • Protein: 40g

ingredients

Chicken breasts (boneless and skinless)

Cherry tomatoes, halved

Fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced

Fresh basil leaves

Balsamic vinegar

Olive oil

Salt and pepper

tools

Large skillet or frying pan

Oven-safe baking dish (if finishing in the oven)

Cooking utensils (spatula, tongs, etc.)


Cabbage and Sausage Stir-Fry

Cabbage and Sausage Stir-Fry is a simple and flavorful dish that combines savory sausage with crispy cabbage and aromatic seasonings. This stir-fry is quick to make and offers a satisfying combination of textures and tastes, making it a perfect weeknight dinner option.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

4-6 servings

instructions

step 1

Prepare Ingredients: Slice the sausage, cabbage, onion, and mince the garlic. Sear Sausage: Heat olive oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the sliced sausage and cook until browned and slightly crispy.

step 2

Cook Onion and Garlic: Add the sliced onion to the skillet with the sausage. Cook until softened and translucent, then add minced garlic and cook until fragrant.

step 3

Add Cabbage: Add the thinly sliced cabbage to the skillet. Stir-fry for a few minutes until the cabbage starts to wilt but remains crisp-tender.

step 4

Season and Finish: Drizzle soy sauce and rice vinegar over the cabbage and sausage mixture. Season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes (if using). Stir well to combine and cook for another minute or two until heated through.

Cabbage and Sausage Stir-Fry calories & nutrition facts :


  • Calories: 280 per serving
  • Total Fat: 20g
  • Saturated Fat: 7g
  • Cholesterol: 60mg
  • Sodium: 830mg
  • Total Carbohydrates: 10g
  • Dietary Fiber: 4g
  • Sugars: 5g
  • Protein: 18g

ingredients

Smoked sausage or kielbasa, sliced

Green cabbage, thinly sliced

Onion, thinly sliced

Garlic, minced

Soy sauce (or tamari for gluten-free)

Rice vinegar

Olive oil or vegetable oil

Salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes (optional)

tools

Large skillet or wok

Cooking utensils (spatula, tongs, etc.)


Greek Salad with Grilled Chicken

Greek Salad with Grilled Chicken is a refreshing and nutritious dish that combines crisp vegetables, creamy feta cheese, olives, and a tangy dressing, topped with tender grilled chicken. This salad is packed with Mediterranean flavors and makes for a satisfying and healthy meal.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

12 min.

serving

2-4 servings

instructions

step 1

Prepare the Chicken: Season chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and a drizzle of olive oil. Grill over medium-high heat for 5-6 minutes per side or until cooked through. Let the chicken rest before slicing.

step 2

Assemble the Salad: In a large bowl, combine chopped romaine lettuce, sliced cucumber, halved cherry tomatoes, thinly sliced red onion, and pitted Kalamata olives.

step 3

Add Grilled Chicken: Slice the grilled chicken breasts and arrange them on top of the salad.

step 4

Top with Feta Cheese and Dressing: Sprinkle crumbled feta cheese over the salad. Drizzle Greek salad dressing over the entire salad.

step 5

Garnish and Serve: Garnish with fresh parsley or oregano. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Greek Salad with Grilled Chicken calories & nutrition facts :


  • Calories: 290 per serving
  • Total Fat: 12g
  • Saturated Fat: 5g
  • Cholesterol: 80mg
  • Sodium: 750mg
  • Total Carbohydrates: 10g
  • Dietary Fiber: 3g
  • Sugars: 5g
  • Protein: 35g

ingredients

Chicken breasts (boneless and skinless)

Romaine lettuce, chopped

Cucumber, sliced

Cherry tomatoes, halved

Red onion, thinly sliced

Kalamata olives, pitted

Feta cheese, crumbled

Fresh parsley or oregano (for garnish)

Greek salad dressing (olive oil, lemon juice, red wine vinegar, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper)

tools

Grill pan or outdoor grill

Mixing bowls

Tongs or spatula for grilling

Serving platter or plates


Cauliflower Crust Pizza

Cauliflower Crust Pizza is a low-carb alternative to traditional pizza crust, using finely grated cauliflower as the base. This gluten-free and keto-friendly pizza crust is topped with your favorite pizza toppings for a delicious and healthier pizza option.

prep time

25 min.

cook time

40 min.

serving

3 servings

instructions

step 1

Prepare Cauliflower Crust: Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C). Grate the cauliflower using a food processor or box grater. Steam the grated cauliflower until tender, then allow it to cool. In a mixing bowl, combine the cooled cauliflower with eggs, grated Parmesan cheese, shredded mozzarella cheese, and Italian seasoning. Mix until well combined.

step 2

Form the Crust: Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Spread the cauliflower mixture onto the parchment paper, shaping it into a round pizza crust, about 1/4-inch thick.

step 3

Bake the Crust: Bake the cauliflower crust in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until golden and crispy around the edges.

step 4

Add Toppings and Bake Again: Remove the crust from the oven. Spread pizza sauce over the crust, then sprinkle with shredded cheese and your favorite toppings. Return the pizza to the oven and bake for an additional 10-12 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

step 5

Serve and Enjoy: Once cooked, slice the cauliflower crust pizza into wedges. Serve hot and enjoy!

Cauliflower Crust Pizza calories & nutrition facts :


  • Calories: 250 per serving
  • Total Fat: 15g
  • Saturated Fat: 7g
  • Cholesterol: 60mg
  • Sodium: 600mg
  • Total Carbohydrates: 10g
  • Dietary Fiber: 3g
  • Sugars: 3g
  • Protein: 20g

ingredients

Cauliflower, grated or riced

Eggs

Parmesan cheese, grated

Mozzarella cheese, shredded

Italian seasoning (optional)

Pizza sauce

Shredded cheese (such as mozzarella)

Your choice of pizza toppings (e.g., pepperoni, bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, etc.)

tools

Food processor or box grater

Mixing bowl

Baking sheet or pizza stone

Parchment paper

Oven preheated to 425°F (220°C)


Baked Salmon with Lemon and Dill

Baked Salmon with Lemon and Dill is a quick and flavorful dish that highlights the natural richness of salmon. Seasoned with fresh dill, lemon slices, salt, and pepper, the salmon is baked until tender and flaky. This recipe is perfect for a light and healthy meal, served alongside your favorite low-carb sides. Enjoy the vibrant flavors of lemon and dill in this simple and satisfying dish

prep time

10 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

2 servings

instructions

step 1

Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

step 2

Prepare the Salmon: Place the salmon fillets in a baking dish. If using skin-on salmon, place them skin-side down. Season the salmon with salt and pepper.

step 3

Add Lemon and Dill: Arrange lemon slices on top of the salmon fillets. Sprinkle chopped fresh dill (or dried dill) over the salmon. Drizzle a little olive oil over the top for moisture and flavor.

step 4

Cover with Foil and Bake: Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil, sealing it around the edges. Bake the salmon in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork. Cooking time may vary depending on the thickness of your salmon fillets.

step 5

Serve and Enjoy: Carefully remove the foil from the baking dish. Transfer the baked salmon fillets to serving plates. Garnish with additional fresh dill and lemon slices if desired. Serve the baked salmon with a side of steamed vegetables or a salad for a complete low-carb meal.

Baked Salmon with Lemon and Dill calories & nutrition facts :


  • Calories: 320 per serving
  • Total Fat: 20g
  • Saturated Fat: 4g
  • Cholesterol: 85mg
  • Sodium: 420mg
  • Total Carbohydrates: 2g
  • Dietary Fiber: 0g
  • Sugars: 0g
  • Protein: 30g

ingredients

2 salmon fillets (about 6 ounces each), skin-on or skinless

1 lemon, thinly sliced

Fresh dill, chopped (or use dried dill)

Salt and pepper, to taste

Olive oil

tools

Baking dish

Aluminum foil

Oven preheated to 400°F (200°C)
